Privacy Policy

S.A.M – Skabo App Music

Effective Date: 15 December 2025

Summary: S.A.M – Skabo App Music does not collect, store, track, or share any user data. The App contains no analytics, no ads, and no in-app purchases.

Overview

S.A.M – Skabo App Music (“the App”) is a free music player application developed to provide access to music by the artist Skabo. The App is distributed via the Apple App Store and is intended primarily for users in Serbia.

We are committed to protecting user privacy. The App is built with a privacy-first approach and does not collect, track, store, or share any user data.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data or usage data.

This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, etc.)
  • IP addresses or network information
  • Location data (precise or approximate)
  • Usage data, diagnostics, or crash reports
  • Cookies or tracking technologies

The App does not require user registration or login.

App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

The App does not track users and does not use tracking technologies as defined by Apple. Therefore, App Tracking Transparency permission is not requested.

Analytics, Advertising, and Monetization

The App contains:

  • No analytics (including Firebase or third-party tools)
  • No advertising
  • No in-app purchases
  • No subscriptions

The App is completely free to use.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party SDKs or services that collect data. Music content included in the App is distributed with explicit permission from the artist (Skabo).

Data Sharing

Because no data is collected, no data is shared with third parties, service providers, advertisers, or analytics platforms.

Children’s Privacy

The App does not collect any personal data from users of any age, including children under the age of 13.

Data Security

Since the App does not collect or store user data, there is no user data to secure or expose.

User Rights

Because no personal data is collected or processed, there is no data to access, correct, export, or delete. If you have questions, you may contact us using the information below.
